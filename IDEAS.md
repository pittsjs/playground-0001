# Game Ideas

Brainstormed concepts for this project. The current direction is **Basketball Pinball**, but these are saved for future reference.

## Current: Basketball Pinball

A pinball table with a basketball theme. Flippers, bumpers, hoops as targets, combo multipliers, and an "On Fire" mode. High-score chasing arcade game.

## Alternative Game Concepts

### Gameplay Additions (to any base game)
- "On Fire" mechanic (NBA Jam style) -- make 3 in a row for temporary boost + flame trail
- Pump fake -- tap Space to fake, hold to shoot
- Dunks -- sprint close to basket for a slam with screen shake
- Shot clock -- 24-second timer per possession
- Stamina bar -- sprinting drains energy that regenerates
- Block shots -- timed key press on defense to swat shots

### Game Modes
- 3-Point Contest -- timed shooting from 5 spots around the arc
- H-O-R-S-E -- take turns making trick shots from specific spots
- Tournament -- bracket of AI opponents with increasing difficulty
- Practice / Free Shoot -- no opponent, just you and the hoop

### Full Game Pivots
- **1v1 Half Court** (v1.0.0) -- already built, committed in git
- **Arcade Mini-Game Collection** -- multiple basketball mini-games in one app (shootout, dunk contest, HORSE, dribbling obstacle course)
- **Trick Shot Puzzle Game** -- aim through obstacles, walls, bouncing surfaces, wind zones; Angry Birds meets basketball
- **Street Ball Story / Roguelike** -- travel to different courts with unique rules/modifiers, earn upgrades between games, lose and start over
- **Basketball GM / Sim** -- draft players, build roster, set strategies, simulate a season
- **Local Multiplayer Party Game** -- two players on one keyboard (WASD vs arrows), mini-games like "first to 5 threes" or "knockout"
- **Training / Rhythm Game** -- drill-based gameplay with timing mechanics, score based on precision
- **Physics Sandbox** -- open court, adjustable gravity/bounce/spin, place ramps/trampolines/portals, make ridiculous shots
- **Turn-Based Tactics** -- grid-based court, move players and pick actions (pass, screen, shoot, steal) each turn; chess meets basketball
- **Basketball Pinball** -- (current direction) half-court as a pinball table with flippers, bumpers, ramps, hoops
- **Vertical Dunk Runner** -- side-scrolling endless runner, tap to jump and dunk through hoops at various heights
- **Basketball Pool / Billiards** -- top-down, aim and "cue" the basketball to bank off walls into hoops; geometry puzzle meets competition
- **Zero-Gravity Basketball** -- space setting, jetpacks, wrapping court edges, totally different physics

### Visual / Polish Ideas
- Player sprites instead of circles (simple 2-3 frame animations)
- Court themes -- unlock different courts (playground, arena, rooftop, neon)
- Instant replay -- slow-mo on buzzer beaters or threes
- Particle upgrades -- sneaker dust, net ripple on swish, confetti on game winner
- Commentator text -- dynamic play-by-play ("He's heating up!", "From downtown!")

### Progression / Meta
- Stats tracking -- shooting %, wins, highest-scoring game (localStorage)
- AI difficulty tiers -- Rookie / Pro / Legend
- Player customization -- pick color, adjust speed vs. shooting attributes

### Wild / Unique
- Power-ups that spawn on court (magnet ball, giant hoop, freeze defender, 2x points)
- Weather effects on outdoor courts (rain = more turnovers, wind = shot drift)
- Signature moves -- unlock special abilities after win streaks
